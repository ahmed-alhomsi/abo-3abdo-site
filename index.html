Our Story

We are a restaurant offering Lebanese food and mezza. Abu-3abdo was founded year 2014 providing high quality fruits and vegetables, juices, Meat, Chicken, BBQ, and bakery. It began with a small store of fresh fruits and vegetables year 1997, after 23 years the owner decided to expand the company reach more customers so he was the first to introduced a restaurant and a market offering varieties of food at the same time.
